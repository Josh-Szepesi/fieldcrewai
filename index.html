FAQ

Questions? We've Got Answers.

How much does this cost?

It depends on what you need. Our SEO content packages start at a flat monthly rate. Lead automation and operations are scoped based on your business. We'll give you a clear quote on our strategy call — no surprises, no hidden fees.

Do I need a website already?

It helps, but it's not required. If you have a website, we can work with it. If you don't, we can help you get set up with something simple and effective. Either way, we'll make sure your online presence is working for you.

How long until I see results?

Lead automation starts working immediately — missed call text-back and follow-ups can be live within days. SEO content takes longer — typically 60-90 days to see meaningful ranking improvements. We'll set realistic expectations upfront.

What trades do you work with?

We specialize in high-ticket home services: roofing, HVAC, plumbing, and electrical. These are the trades where our systems deliver the most impact. If you're in a related trade, reach out — we may be able to help.

Do I have to do anything?

Almost nothing. We need about 30 minutes upfront to learn about your business. After that, we handle everything. You'll get a monthly report and can check in anytime — but you won't need to lift a finger on the marketing or tech side.

What if I already use a CRM or other tools?

That's fine. We work with what you have. Whether it's ServiceTitan, Jobber, Housecall Pro, or a spreadsheet — we'll integrate with your existing tools or recommend something better if it makes sense. No ripping and replacing unless it's clearly worth it.
