Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

When you contact Field Crew AI, we collect the following information to respond to your inquiry and provide our services:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Your business information (trade, website, role)
  • Any message or inquiry you send us through our contact form
  • Call and SMS data if you reach out directly by phone or text

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiry promptly
  • Schedule and conduct Discovery Calls and strategy assessments
  • Set up systems and automations specific to your business
  • Provide ongoing support and account management
  • Improve our service and operations based on feedback

We do not use your information for marketing purposes beyond confirming scheduled calls and providing service updates related to your engagement.

Text Messaging

If you provide your phone number on this site, you consent to receive SMS messages related to your inquiry. Message and data rates may apply.

You may reply STOP at any time to opt out of text messages. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

We use HubSpot as our internal CRM system to manage client relationships and communication. HubSpot stores your information securely and is bound by the same confidentiality and privacy commitments we follow.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, and disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
