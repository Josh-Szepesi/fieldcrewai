# Operations Audit

**Price:** Contact Josh to scope (no published price)
**Length:** 90+ minutes, multi-session if needed
**Booking:** Email Josh@fieldcrewai.com or use /contact

## Who It Is For
Contractors doing $2M or more in annual revenue with existing systems in place: CRM, sales reps, admin staff, ad spend, multiple crews. The question is not whether there are gaps. It is which ones are costing the most.

## What We Look At
- CRM utilization and pipeline data across the team
- Ad spend and lead source attribution by channel
- Sales rep follow-up consistency and timing
- Estimate aging and pipeline leakage
- Team workflow and handoffs (sales to field, field to admin)
- Review volume and process
- Reporting and metrics accuracy

## What the Client Leaves With
- A multi-layer lead flow map
- Prioritized fix list with ROI framing
- CRM utilization recommendations specific to their platform
- Ad spend and source analysis
- Clear next steps for the highest-value improvements

## Relevant CRMs
AccuLynx, Jobber, ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, and others. The audit is platform-specific, not generic.
