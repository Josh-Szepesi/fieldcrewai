# Efficiency Assessment

**Price:** $497 one-time
**Length:** 45 minutes (may extend to 60)
**Booking:** Calendly — https://calendly.com/josh-fieldcrewai/strategy

## Who It Is For
Any contractor at any stage. Explicitly includes early-stage and solo operators. Does not require an existing CRM, sales team, or admin staff.

## What We Look At
- Website and contact flow
- Phone handling and missed calls
- After-hours response
- Form submissions
- CRM, spreadsheet, inbox, or lead tracking
- Quote and estimate follow-up
- Review request process
- Google Business Profile and local visibility basics
- Repeated manual admin tasks
- Tools already in use
- Places where the business depends too much on owner memory

## What the Client Leaves With
- A ranked fix list: do now, do later, ignore for now
- Which fixes are free using tools they already have
- Which fixes Josh can help implement, only if they want help
- A plain-English next step for missed calls, estimate follow-up, reviews, and lead tracking

## Satisfaction Commitment
If the client does not leave with at least one clear fix worth more than $497, Josh will make it right.

## Implementation Options After
- Free fixes: process changes and unused CRM settings turned on during the session
- Ongoing services: missed-call text-back, monthly blog content (~$100/month)
- AI phone coverage: setup fee + ~$200-300/month (varies by volume and configuration)
- Project work: website, CRM migration, or deeper automation (quoted after assessment)
- Nothing automatic. Nothing bundled without agreement.

## Positioning Notes
- This is a diagnostic product, not a consulting retainer
- Do not pitch this as "step one of a six-month engagement"
- DIY implementation is explicitly available and encouraged when appropriate
- The assessment often finds fixes that cost $0 and take 20 minutes to set up
