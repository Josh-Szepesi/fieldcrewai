# Site Reviews — 2026-05-03

Three persona reviews conducted as part of the initial site build and refinement session.

---

## Jim (Established Contractor, Roofing, Saskatoon)

**Score:** 8/10

### What Works
- The diagnostic-first framing builds trust with a skeptical buyer
- $497 price is not an obstacle for Jim
- The FAQ "Is this a sales call? No." reduces friction meaningfully
- The contractor lead journey diagram demonstrates process fluency

### What Does Not Work
- No social proof from contractors at his scale
- Implementation cost question was unanswered (now fixed)
- Satisfaction guarantee was buried in FAQ (now prominent)

### Changes Made
- Satisfaction commitment added before hero CTA and near Calendly
- Implementation cost FAQ rewritten with specific pricing
- Anti-upsell trust strip added to hero
- Placeholder testimonials replaced with real assessment examples
- Established-contractor callout section added before About Josh

### Open Items
- Real testimonial from a crew-based roofing contractor needed
- Pricing page or implementation cost breakdown could be clearer

---

## Ethan (Early-Stage HVAC, 8 Months In)

**Score:** 8.5/10

### What Works
- Hero hits hard — he is living the missed-call problem daily
- Stage selector "Just starting out" tab speaks directly to his situation
- FAQ satisfaction commitment reduces his booking risk
- Anti-upsell trust strip addresses his primary anxiety

### What Does Not Work
- No testimonial or example from a 1-2 person operation
- DIY implementation path was not explicit (now added to FAQ)
- "Done-with-you" language absent (partially addressed)

### Changes Made
- Small-operator callout added ("if it is mostly you and one helper")
- Specific free fixes listed for early-stage operators
- DIY FAQ added
- "What you leave with" updated to include "which fixes are free using tools you already have"

### Open Items
- Real example from a solo or 2-person HVAC/plumbing/roofing operator needed
- "Done-with-you" implementation path not yet explicitly offered on site

---

## Kathy (Multi-Crew Roofing, $5.2M Revenue)

**Score:** 6.5/10

### What Works
- "Diagnostic first" approach is the right instinct for her
- Operations Audit tier card gives her a self-selection path
- The "Running $2M+" stage tab covers her adequately
- AI phone section "pilot-first" framing is the only reason she would consider it

### What Does Not Work
- $497 price signals beginner-tier to someone used to paying $5k-15k for consulting
- Hero language sounds like a one-truck message
- Several problems in the problems section are irrelevant to her (admin handles missed calls)
- 45-minute framing undersells the depth available

### Changes Made
- Operations Audit tier card exists and links to /contact
- "Running $2M+" stage tab with relevant examples
- Established-contractor section names AccuLynx, Jobber, ServiceTitan

### Open Items
- Case study from a multi-crew business needed for Kathy to convert
- Higher-tier pricing and scope needs to be defined
- Consider a dedicated "for $2M+ operations" landing page eventually

---

## Strategic Note
Jim and Ethan are the primary ICPs for the homepage. Kathy is a secondary path — she has a credible Operations Audit route but the homepage is not optimized around her yet. That is intentional.
